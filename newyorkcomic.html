INVISIBLE THREATS IN AIR

SUMMARY
OF
THE
STORY

In New York City, tracking greenhouse gas emissions is a community effort involving pilots, satellite builders, modeling experts, students, city leaders, and residents. With support from NYSERDA, NOAA, and the US GHG Center, the goal is to improve emissions estimates by combining sensor data across the city with NASA satellite and airborne data. This collaboration helps the city work toward its 2050 carbon neutral target.
